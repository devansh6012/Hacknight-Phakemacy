Protection against fraud medicines

Check for medicines

Idea behind our
Project

Estimates say that counterfeit drugs provide approximately $75 billion in revenue annually to illegal operators and have caused more than 100,000 deaths worldwide.
To tackle this problem, we have implemented a system to detect counterfeit medicines during its delivery only, and also reduce a substantial amount of time spent in ordering medicines online by a hospital.
This project has been implemented using HTML5, CSS, Javascript, Crust and Polygon
